Migration Analysis: YPF → Axcera

Cost evaluation, contractual risks and scenarios for Futures expansion

Date: April 2026 Prepared for: NEOMAAA Executive Team Version: 1.0
Latest Update — Contract Negotiation v2
What changed with the updated counterproposal
14 Items · 4 Red Lines

The economic structure (pricing, Year 1, Year 2) did not move. What moved is risk exposure. We went from a 12-month locked agreement with no exit, no SLA enforcement, no data guarantee and vague fees — to a contract with hard caps, exit rights and binding technical commitments.

×
Before — Original Axcera
  • 12-month lock-in, no exit right
  • $10K permanent revenue trigger
  • Advance payment 3 months at Axcera's discretion
  • 18% annual late interest
  • Resumption fee uncapped
  • 2-year non-compete, blanket scope
  • No SLA — "AS IS"
  • No data return mechanism
  • Verbal technical promises (non-binding)
  • Auto-renewal 60 days notice
✓
After — Counterproposal v2
  • Exit at any time — 30 days notice + 1 month fee
  • Tiered pricing with monthly reset at $500K
  • Advance payment only after 2 missed + 10 BD cure
  • Late interest capped at 8%
  • Resumption fee removed entirely
  • Non-compete 6 months, direct IP replication only
  • 99.5% uptime SLA + 4-hr response + credits
  • Full data export in 14 days, no charge
  • Binding technical annex (APIs, webhooks, PSPs, AWS)
  • Auto-renewal reduced to 30 days notice
Bottom line: Same $690K over 2 years — but worst-case downside dropped from unbounded ($1M+ and stuck) to ~$60K to exit at any time. See Section 12 for the full mathematical audit.

6 Critical Contract Clauses — Axcera

Non-Compete — 2 Years Post-ContractCritical
Section 6.3
The Licensee may not directly or indirectly develop, create, license or offer any platform that competes with Axcera, during the contract and for 2 years after termination.

Impact: If they terminate with Axcera, they cannot use another prop trading technology provider for 2 years. Potentially business-destroying.
Permanent Revenue Trigger (+$10K/mo)Critical
Appendix A, item (c)
If revenue exceeds $250,000 in any single month, an additional $10,000/month is applied in perpetuity, regardless of whether revenue subsequently declines.

Impact: Triggered immediately at current revenue levels. Additional cost: $120,000/year for life. One-way ratchet with no reversal.
Software "AS IS" — No WarrantiesHigh
Article VII
Software delivered "AS IS" without warranty of any kind. Axcera does not guarantee it will be bug-free, uninterrupted, or error-free.

Impact: Paying $30K+/month with no SLA, no guaranteed uptime, and no support commitment during critical outages.
IP 100% Owned by AxceraHigh
Article VI + Article X
All technology and all original materials are the exclusive property of Axcera. Including customizations paid for by NEOMAAA.

Impact: Everything paid at $100/hr becomes Axcera's property. Upon termination, you leave empty-handed.
Discretionary Advance Payment (3 months)High
Section 3.2(c)
Axcera may, at its sole discretion, require up to 3 months in advance if it determines "financial risk." 5 business days to pay.

Impact: They can demand $75,000–$97,500 within 5 days with no objective criteria.
Immediate Termination Without NoticeMedium
Section 9.2
Axcera may terminate and revoke credentials immediately, without notice, for "illegal or unethical activities." Vague and subjective definition.

Impact: They can cut service overnight without due process.
Unilateral Limitation of LiabilityMedium
Article VII
Axcera is not liable for indirect, consequential damages, loss of profits, or business interruption. Maximum liability: repair or replace the software.

Impact: If a failure causes losses to traders, Axcera assumes no financial responsibility.
